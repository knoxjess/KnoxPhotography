About Me

About Me

Hi there! I'm Austin Knox, based in Nashville, TN.

With four years of experience in photography, I have developed a deep passion for capturing moments through my lens.

I am also a proud father of twin girls. This role has inspired me to preserve precious memories, driving my enthusiasm for photography even further.

While photography is currently my part-time pursuit, my goal is to turn it into a full-time profession. I am dedicated to improving my skills and sharing the beauty I see with others.

My Services

My Services

Offering professional and personalized photography services to capture your special moments and needs.

Wedding Photography

Capturing the special moments of a wedding day, including pre-ceremony preparations, the ceremony itself, and the reception.
Services Offered: Full-day coverage, engagement sessions, bridal portraits, high-resolution digital images.
Target Clients: Engaged couples looking for professional documentation of their wedding day.

Portrait Photography

Creating personalized and artistic portraits of individuals, couples, families, or groups.
Services Offered: Studio sessions, outdoor sessions, corporate headshots, family portraits, senior photos, and retouching services.
Target Clients: Families, individuals, professionals, and high school seniors.

Event Photography

Covering various types of events such as corporate functions, birthday parties, concerts, and festivals.
Services Offered: On-site photography, candid shots, group photos, photo booths, and same-day digital delivery.
Target Clients: Event planners, corporations, individuals hosting private events, and non-profit organizations.

Maternity Photography

Documenting the beauty of pregnancy and the early days of a newborn's life.
Services Offered: Maternity sessions, newborn sessions, milestone sessions, themed shoots.
Target Clients: Expectant mothers, new parents, and families celebrating new additions.
